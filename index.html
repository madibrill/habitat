About

  • What is Habitat?

    Habitat is a Christian based ministry that helps our community by building strength, stability, and self-reliance in partnership with families in need of decent and affordable housing. Habitat homeowners help build their own homes alongside volunteers and pay an affordable mortgage.

  • What is a Chapter?

    We are a group of students who are passionate about helping people build better lives for themselves. We are a student-led organization that partners with Habitat for Humanity of Kosciusko County to fulfill the four functions of a campus chapter: direct service, educating, advocating, and fundraising.

  • What is a Partner Family?

    Partner Family" is the term Habitat uses for the family for whom they are building a home. Partner is an essential word in Habitat language. It means that we are in this together and together we build homes for families in need.

Services

Direct Service

We partner with HFHKC by volunteering on new home constructions, rehabilitations, rehabs, neighborhood revitalizations, ReStore and office tasks.

Fundraising

We raise funds to support the work of HFHKC. These funds are used to build a home for the family we choose to partner with during the school year.

Advocating

As leaders and members, we must advocate for affordable housing because building alone will not end poverty housing. Being a Habitat advocate means raising our voice to shape policies to serve people in need of decent housing.

Educating

We educate others on our campus and our community of Kosciusko County about the importance and need for strong, stable housing and what Habitat does to increase access to housing solutions.

Melisa Francis

During the 2018-2019 school year, this chapter will partner with Habitat for Humanity of Kosciusko County by focusing its efforts on supporting Melisa Francis (known as Missy). She, like all Habitat partner families, is waiting for her home to be built. We will spend time throughout the school year getting to know Missy. It is also our hope to do what we can through our fundraising initiatives to financially contribute to her build.
